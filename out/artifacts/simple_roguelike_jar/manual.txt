The goal of the game is to get to the fifth floor of the dungeon and slay the evil grand master wizard, Richard Nixon.

At level 4, you have the choice of learning either Ice or Fire.
Ice can deal 3-6 base damage.
Fire deals 5 base damage.

At level 9, you have the choice of learning either Ice+ or Fire+.
Ice+ can deal 5-13 base damage.
Fireball+ can deal 9 base damage.

The game saves a text file for every character you die with.
